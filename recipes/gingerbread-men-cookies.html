Gingerbread Men Cookies

Description

Embark on a sweet journey with our delightful Gingerbread Men Cookies – a festive treat that captures the warm and spicy essence of the holiday season. Rolled and cut into classic gingerbread man shapes, each cookie is baked to golden perfection, creating a perfect balance of crispy edges and chewy centers. Decorate these charming treats with sweet icing details and colorful sprinkles to add a touch of holiday magic. Whether enjoyed on their own or as a creative addition to your holiday cookie exchange, these Gingerbread Men Cookies are sure to fill your home with the sweet aroma of seasonal joy.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour, plus more for rolling
  • 2 teaspoons McCormick® Ginger, Ground
  • 1 teaspoon McCormick® Cinnamon, Ground
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon McCormick® Nutmeg, Ground
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup butter, softened
  • ¾ cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • ½ cup molasses
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon McCormick® Pure Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Mix flour, ginger, cinnamon, baking soda, nutmeg, and salt in large bowl.
  2. Beat butter and brown sugar in large bowl with electric mixer on medium speed until light and fluffy. Add molasses, egg, and vanilla; mix well. Gradually beat in flour mixture on low speed until well mixed.
  3. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  4. Roll dough on a lightly floured surface to a thickness of 1/4 inch. Cut into gingerbread men shapes with a 5-inch cookie cutter. Place gingerbread men 1 inch apart on ungreased baking sheets.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until edges of cookies are set and just begin to brown, 8 to 10 minutes. Cool on baking sheets for 1 to 2 minutes, then remove to wire racks to cool completely.
  6. Decorate cooled cookies as desired. Store cookies in airtight container up to 5 days.
